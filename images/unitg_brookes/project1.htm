    Our first project Home:Comfort culminated on Thursday with on site presentations. Each student began with an object that reminded them of home, and went on to create an intervention for our site, Little Portugal, in London. The interventions combined elements of the students’ chosen objects, with findings from site and the local community. The above is a selection of the presented work including a seat for a bus stop, a scone making worktop, a conversation space and a study of the site through lacemaking.
